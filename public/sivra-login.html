What is Sivra

Your store. Your server.
Your rules.

Sivra is a self-hosted e-commerce platform that gives merchants the power to build and run online stores — without paying platform fees, without being dependent on a third-party, and without the risk of being shut down.

Built for independence

Host Sivra on any server you choose. AWS, DigitalOcean, your own machine — you decide. No one can take your store offline.

Designed for merchants

Everything a merchant needs — products, orders, collections, pages, customers — in a clean, easy-to-use dashboard.

Monetized from day one

Sivra charges merchants a simple monthly subscription. You run the platform, they run their stores, everyone wins.

How it works

Up and running in four steps

1

Deploy Sivra

Install Sivra on any server or cloud provider. Takes under 10 minutes with our setup guide.

2

Invite merchants

Merchants sign up, create their store, and start adding products — all within your platform.

3

They sell, you earn

Each merchant pays a monthly subscription fee. You keep 100% of it — Sivra takes nothing.

4

Scale freely

Add more merchants, expand features, upgrade your server — grow at your own pace.
